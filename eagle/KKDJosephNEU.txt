BRPBot. a bot that will support keeper (KP) when you are playing the BRP game. 
The bot will record users’ information, users’ choice, help users to roll the dice, play the background music, 
show the pictures related to the background story, send secret messages to the users. 

What is BRP games: A table top role playing game. Players will create their roles under limited resources. 
They will use their skills and roll dices to finish their missions. Like Call of Cthuluh(COC), Dungeons & Dragons(D&D).

What is KP: Keeper, Dungeon maste(DM), the master of the game, 
the one who help all the players continue game process but not one of the players.
